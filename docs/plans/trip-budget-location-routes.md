## 🏆 My Top 5 Recommendations for Your Group (March 2026)

### 1. 🥇 Rajasthan Full Circuit (BEST VALUE — Prakhar's home turf!)
**Why:** Prakhar knows the state, saves ₹2,000–3,000 on accommodation at start, March is the last good month before summer. Full heritage + desert + wildlife in one trip.  
**Cost:** ~₹27,000–31,000/pp · **Duration:** 8–9 days

### 2. 🥈 Meghalaya + Assam (Most Adventurous)
**Why:** Living root bridges, crystal river at Dawki, rhinos at Kaziranga. March is perfect before monsoon. Heavily underrated by most travellers.  
**Cost:** ~₹25,000–38,000/pp · **Duration:** 6–7 days

### 3. 🥉 Kerala Backwaters + Hills (Most Relaxing)
**Why:** Houseboat split 3-ways is incredible value, tea plantations in Munnar, Fort Kochi art scene. March is warm but still manageable.  
**Cost:** ~₹22,000–35,000/pp · **Duration:** 5–6 days

### 4. 🏅 Hampi + Gokarna + Goa (Heritage + Beach Combo)
**Why:** UNESCO ruins + pristine beaches + Goa nightlife. Fly into Goa, bus to Gokarna, bus to Hampi, fly out from Hubli. Great value circuit.  
**Cost:** ~₹18,000–28,000/pp · **Duration:** 6–7 days

### 5. 🏅 Gulmarg Snow + Amritsar (Last Snow of the Season)
**Why:** March is the last month for snow activities at Gulmarg. Combine with Golden Temple in Amritsar on the way. Unique skiing + spirituality combo.  
**Cost:** ~₹18,000–30,000/pp · **Duration:** 4–5 days
